AI Ops brings conversational AI to the command console. Ask questions about your fleet in natural language and get instant answers backed by real operational data.

**Capabilities:**
- Natural language queries: "What's the readiness status of Squadron 12?" or "Show me fuel consumption trends for the last 30 days"
- Automated report generation from conversational prompts
- Intelligent alert triage — AI categorizes and prioritizes incoming alerts, surfacing critical items first
- Anomaly explanation — when the system detects something unusual, AI Ops explains what it found and why it matters
- Watch handover briefs — automatically generated situation summaries for incoming watch officers

**How it works:**
AI Ops has read access to fleet data, sensor logs, and crew records. It can query, filter, and summarize this data in response to natural language questions. It cannot take command actions — it's a research and analysis tool, not an autonomous agent.

**Privacy and security:**
All AI processing happens within the AEGIS security boundary. No fleet data is sent to external services. Conversation logs are retained for audit purposes and can be reviewed by security officers.

AI Ops is available now on all tiers. Try it from the sidebar — just click "AI Ops" and start asking questions.
