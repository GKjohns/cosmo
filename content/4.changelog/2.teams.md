Joint operations just got significantly easier. This release introduces multi-fleet coordination — the ability to link multiple fleet groups into a shared operational picture.

**What's new:**
- Shared tactical overlays between allied fleet commands
- Unified sensor picture with configurable data sharing levels
- Cross-fleet formation management and transition coordination
- Role-based access control for joint operations staff
- Automated deconfliction of operating areas and patrol zones
- Joint communications channels with per-fleet encryption keys

**How it works:**
Fleet commanders can create coordination groups and invite allied commands. Each participant controls exactly what data they share — from full sensor feeds to sanitized position reports. AEGIS handles the data fusion automatically, presenting a coherent combined picture to all participants.

This capability was validated during the Kepler Station joint exercise, where three allied fleets maintained full tactical coordination through AEGIS for 72 continuous hours.
