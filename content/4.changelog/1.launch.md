AEGIS Fleet Command Operations Platform is now live and operational. This initial release represents months of development in close collaboration with fleet commanders and operations staff across multiple theaters.

From the outset, we focused on the things that matter most in fleet operations: reliability, speed, and clarity of information. Whether you're managing a patrol group or coordinating a full battle group, AEGIS scales to meet your requirements.

**Launch capabilities:**
- Real-time vessel tracking and status monitoring across all assigned sectors
- Integrated sensor fusion from radar, lidar, and passive EM arrays
- Crew management with watch rotation tracking and certification records
- Encrypted fleet-wide communications with configurable EMCON levels
- Responsive command console optimized for both desktop and bridge displays
- Comprehensive documentation and onboarding guides

**Architecture highlights:**
We've built AEGIS on a distributed architecture designed for resilience. Edge nodes maintain local operation when relay connectivity is degraded, and all data synchronizes automatically when contact is restored.

This is just the beginning. Your operational feedback will directly shape the platform's evolution. Report issues, request capabilities, and help us build the fleet management system the service deserves.
