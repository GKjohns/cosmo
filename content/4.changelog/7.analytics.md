Data-driven command decisions require data you can actually use. The new Fleet Analytics Dashboard transforms raw operational data into actionable intelligence for fleet commanders and planning staff.

**Available analytics:**
- Mission success and completion rates with trend analysis
- Crew readiness scores across all vessels and departments
- Sensor performance and reliability metrics by type and platform
- Fuel consumption and logistics efficiency tracking
- Watch rotation effectiveness and fatigue correlation
- Communication volume and latency trends
- Maintenance scheduling optimization recommendations

**Configurable views:**
Every commander sees different things as important. The analytics dashboard supports fully configurable views — choose your metrics, set your time ranges, and save views for quick access. Share views with your staff to ensure everyone is looking at the same picture.

**Export and reporting:**
All analytics can be exported for inclusion in operational reports. Automated weekly digests summarize key trends and flag anomalies that may require attention.
