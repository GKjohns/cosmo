Bridge operations don't stop when the lights go down. This release introduces adaptive display modes designed specifically for low-light environments — night watches, combat conditions, and stealth operations where light discipline matters.

**Display modes:**
- Standard: Full brightness for well-lit operations centers
- Low-light: Reduced brightness with enhanced contrast for night watches
- Combat: Red-shifted display that preserves night vision adaptation
- Stealth: Minimum emission mode for light-discipline environments

**Adaptive behavior:**
AEGIS can automatically transition between display modes based on ambient light sensors or manual override. The transition is smooth — no jarring brightness changes that would disrupt a watch officer's concentration.

**Crew fatigue impact:**
Early adopters report measurably reduced eye strain and improved alertness during extended watch shifts. The low-light mode uses carefully tuned contrast ratios that maintain readability without the visual fatigue caused by standard bright displays in dark environments.
